Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical tour take?

Most tours last between 45-60 minutes, depending on your interests and questions. We're happy to accommodate your schedule and pace.

Can I reschedule my visit?

Absolutely! Just contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled visit, and we'll be happy to arrange a new time that works for you.

Are pets allowed during the tour?

While Goba Peaks is pet-friendly for residents, we ask that you leave pets at home during your initial tour for safety and convenience.

What should I bring?

Just yourself and any questions you have! We'll provide all the information and materials you need during your visit.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have dedicated visitor parking. Your tour guide will provide parking instructions when confirming your appointment.

Can I see multiple apartment types?

Of course! We encourage you to view different apartment types to find the perfect fit for your lifestyle and needs.
